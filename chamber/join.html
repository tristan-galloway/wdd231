Nonprofit Membership

This membership is specifically designed for nonprofit organizations. Benefits include:

  • Free membership with no fees.
  • Access to networking events.
  • Exclusive resources for nonprofits.
×

Bronze Membership

The Bronze membership offers great value with the following benefits:

  • Access to networking events.
  • Business directory listing.
  • Discounts on select chamber-hosted events.
×

Silver Membership

The Silver membership is ideal for businesses looking to expand their reach. Benefits include:

  • All Bronze membership benefits.
  • Priority listing in the business directory.
  • Advertising opportunities on the chamber's website.
  • Exclusive training and workshops.
×

Gold Membership

The Gold membership provides the highest level of benefits, including:

  • All Silver membership benefits.
  • Spotlight position on the chamber's homepage.
  • Free admission to all chamber-hosted events.
  • Customized advertising packages.
